Why Epitome?

I started Epitome Therapy Center along with my like-minded colleague with the mission to address the gaps in child therapy servicesâ€”particularly the lack of a clear, accurate diagnosis and proper guidance for families by offering a holistic, comprehensive approach to child therapy. My goal was to ensure accurate diagnoses and treatments, and provide clear guidance for parents who often feel overwhelmed by the process. Additionally, I was committed to offering a more affordable pricing model for families who face financial difficulties while seeking quality care for their children. I also wanted to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and hands-on expertise to ensure children receive effective, real-world solutions.

Subject matter expertise

As a certified Rehabilitation Therapist and Rehabilitation Psychologist, I have the privilege of delivering occupational therapy, speech and language therapy, physical therapies, conducting clinical assessments, arranging behavior modification programs as well as planning and research in the field of mental health and disability rehabilitation.

My main areas of specialization are assessment, diagnosis and intervention of neurodevelopmental disorders, musculoskeletal conditions, neurological conditions and neuropsychological disorders. I also specializes in holistic developmental assessment of children and adults.

I regularly train teachers and parents to help them work more effectively with children having developmental challenges at school and home.
