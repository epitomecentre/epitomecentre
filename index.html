Centre for Child Therapy and Counselling

Healing Hearts Healing Lives

At Epitome Centre for Child Therapy and Counselling, our approach is primarily cognitive-behavioral and solution-focused. We will look at the interplay among thoughts, feelings and behaviors to explore how negative thought patterns influence behavior and work to change those patterns to provide emotional relief. We help clients develop real-life skills and coping strategies that can last a lifetime.  We can work together to uncover behaviors and perceptions that hold you or your child back from experiencing more happiness and fulfillment, and bring you to a better understanding of yourself and an improvement in the quality of your life and relationships. We invite you to get a glimpse into a typical day at a Epitome Centre, and the impact it can have on your child’s future. Because at Epitome Centre, little wins can make a BIG difference.

Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions? Call or email us and we'll be happy to help.

How do I know if my child needs therapy?

Indicators that your child might need therapy could be persistent sadness, excessive worry, noticeable behavioral changes, sleep problems, academic decline, or social withdrawal.
Who will be involved in the care of my child?

Our Team includes Rehabilitation Psychologist, Occupational Therapist, Early Interventionist, Speech and Language Pathologist, Special Educator and Physiotherapist. They will work closely with you to make sure everyone is on the same page regarding the care of your child
How will I know if my child is improving?

Sometimes, parents notice changes within just a few sessions. For others, it may take a bit longer, and that’s perfectly normal. It can take time to effectively utilize our toolbox of strategies. At the core, we focus on improving communication, increasing self-awareness, managing intense emotions, and understanding the science behind why coping strategies are effective.
What kind of results can I expect from therapy?

Results vary depending on individual goals and needs. Many clients develop improved clarity, motivation, and improved self-confidence within a few months of coaching.
